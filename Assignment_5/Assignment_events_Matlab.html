First Steps

Check out the demo-script EventDetection.m Here it is better to use subplot to generate subplots (instead of tiledlayout), because we want the first three plots below each other on the left, and the last two plots on the right side. And again, you can link axes with linkaxes.

EMG Analysis

  • Use the option Range from the command readmatrix to skip three rows and three columns. The first column read in then represents the forearm, the second the biceps.
